🌙 Maramataka Calendar

A simple, subtle layer of daily guidance and energy tracking, fitting seamlessly into your existing calendar app.

What this is

This is a lightweight calendar feed that places the daily phase of the maramataka smoothly into your calendar. It appears as an all-day event up top, right alongside your meetings and tasks.

Context note: This is a supportive interpretation designed to build awareness and connection. It does not replace mātauranga, override local knowledge, or claim definitive correctness.

📱 How to add it

iPhone / Apple Calendar

  1. Tap one of the buttons above.
  2. A prompt will appear asking to "Subscribe". Tap Subscribe.
  3. Tap Add confirming your choice.

Google Calendar (Desktop)

  1. Right-click a button above and Copy Link Address.
  2. Open Google Calendar.
  3. On the left side, click the + next to "Other calendars".
  4. Select From URL.
  5. Paste the URL and click Add calendar.

Android

We recommend adding the calendar from your Google Calendar on desktop (using the steps above). It will automatically sync to your Android calendar app.

❌ How to remove it

You can opt-out and remove the calendar cleanly at any time.

iPhone

Go to Settings → Calendar → Accounts → Subscribed Calendars → Tap the calendar and choose Delete Account.

Google Calendar

Go to your Google Calendar Settings (Gear icon) → Look for the calendar under Settings for other calendars on the left → Click it and choose Unsubscribe or Remove.
